Wedding Details and Schedule

We'll be getting married in San Francisco California at The Pearl on July 8th at 4PM. Don't miss that part! But wait, there's more! Scroll down for more details about the events.

Wedding Events

8:30PM || attire: Dressy Casual || Mochica (1469 18th St)

We are excited to welcome everyone to our wedding with Peruvian Drinks. Mochica is a great spot at the top of our neighborhood, Potrero.

Saturday, July 8: Ceremony and Reception

4PM || attire: Cocktail Attire || The Pearl (601 19th St)

The ceremony and the reception will take place at the Pearl. Ceremony begins at 4PM. A rooftop cocktail hour and a reception will immediately follow at the same venue. There is very limited parking so please take a Lyft to the venue.

10:30 onwards || attire: don't change || Third Rail (628 20th St)

The reception will be over, but the party won't! At about 10:30, we'll walk over together to a cool local bar to wind down or wind up the party, depending on the mood. It's open til late so stay as long as you want.

Sunday, July 9 Farewell

9a - 11:30a || attire: as you are || Coffee Bar (1890 Bryant St)

Hang out in SF's coolest district with us at one of our favorite coffee spots for breakfast and lunch. We will be there during this time, just come in and find us.
